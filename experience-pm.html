The entire map was built from scratch: designers hand-drew every layer from architectural and urban development plans, which were then sliced into image tiles on the backend, with unit outlines and infrastructure points (entrances, parking, metro) overlaid programmatically.

Coordinated the full custom map pipeline: designers → tile slicing (backend) → unit outline overlay → interactive frontend with filtering by type, size, price, floor, and delivery date
Designed and maintained an update process to keep the map in sync with shifting construction timelines, unit reconfigurations, and new development phases
Led a full redesign of the map interface, managing the design and development cycle while ensuring continuity of the live product
Supported continuous product development: prioritized and delivered iterative improvements based on business needs and user feedback
