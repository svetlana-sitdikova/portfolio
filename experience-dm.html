Project 03

Online Real Estate Transaction Flow

A complex multi-step flow enabling users to purchase an apartment entirely online: personal questionnaire, document generation, digital signing, file uploads, and payment — with deep CRM integration. The original plan was 6 months. When the CPO pushed back, I rebuilt the plan and delivered an MVP in 2.5 months.

6 mo → 2.5 mo
timeline compressed after scope negotiation
Sep '23 – Apr '24
project timeline

What I did

Managed full delivery: scope definition, estimation, planning, team coordination, integration management, testing, release
Renegotiated scope with CPO and product manager under executive pressure — removed non-critical functionality from the MVP to make the timeline realistic without cutting quality
Scaled the team with additional engineers and QA to meet the compressed deadline
Designed parallel workstreams carefully with tech leads — mapped dependencies in advance to prevent teams from blocking each other at critical points
Managed the critical external CRM integration with a third-party team: embedded the CRM team into our workflow and coordinated their work directly — treating them as part of the team, not a vendor at arm's length
Hired a PM toward project end and executed a smooth handover — full context transfer, no disruption to delivery
What I learned

We missed the deadline by about 1.5 weeks. But I made sure stakeholders could see an almost-complete version in staging throughout the final sprint — visible progress, not a black box. Since then I always build transparency into tight timelines: the finish line should never be a surprise.
