Project 0101

Samolet Ecosystem Multi-Team Program

samolet.ru ↗

Delivery across the full Samolet digital ecosystem: high-load website (5M+ MAU), customer account with online property purchases, and internal B2B tools. My role was to make 53 engineers, designers, and PMs aligned, unblocked, and shipping — while the team and product were both growing rapidly in a multi-vendor environment with an outstaff team.

What I did

Coordinated all 6 teams within a Large-Scale Scrum (LeSS) framework — quarterly planning, cross-team dependency management, workload balancing, and priority alignment
Hired 5 project managers and acted as their direct manager: onboarding, mentoring, 1-to-1s, supporting them in building strong team processes
Built release management: twice-weekly releases for the 5M MAU website, release calendar, clear guidelines, new roles, expanded staging environments
Improved incident management — classification, prioritization framework, Kanban for bug tracking, automated dashboards. CRM bugs dropped from ~400 to <30/month
Led site performance improvement from ~7s to <3s via CDN, caching, and Vue 2 → Nuxt 3 migration
Streamlined design processes: standardized review criteria, introduced a design lead role, implemented designer-driven test design before engineering handoff

Project 0303

Online Real Estate Transaction Flow

A complex multi-step flow enabling users to purchase an apartment entirely online: personal questionnaire, document generation, digital signing, file uploads, and payment — with deep CRM integration. The original plan was 6 months. When the CPO pushed back, I rebuilt the plan and delivered an MVP in 2.5 months.

6 months → 2.5

What I did

Managed full delivery: scope definition, estimation, planning, team coordination, integration management, testing, release
Renegotiated scope with CPO and product manager under executive pressure — removed non-critical functionality from MVP to make the timeline realistic
Scaled the team with additional engineers and QA to meet the compressed deadline
Designed parallel workstreams with tech leads — mapped dependencies in advance to prevent teams from blocking each other
Managed the external CRM integration: embedded the CRM team into our workflow and coordinated their work directly
Hired a PM toward project end and executed a smooth handover with full context transfer
